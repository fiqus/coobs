Why to join COOBS?

The Co-operative Social Balance (COOP + BS = COOBS) is a methodology developed by the International Co-operative Alliance, whose objective is to become a tool for socio-economic management that makes it easier for co-operatives to measure themselves and be accountable to their members, in their capacity as owners, managers, users and all other stakeholders who are impacted by their actions in relation to the fulfilment of their own essence or identity, i.e. from the perspective of co-operative values and principles.
